Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Yes, we highly recommend making reservations, especially on weekends and holidays. You can make a reservation by calling us at (555) 123-4567, using our online booking system, or filling out the contact form above.

We have limited street parking available. We recommend using public transportation or rideshare services. The nearest subway station is Canal Street (A, C, E lines), just a 5-minute walk from our restaurant.

Yes, we have several vegetarian options marked on our menu. While we don't have a dedicated vegan menu, our chefs can prepare vegan dishes upon request. Please inform your server about any dietary restrictions when ordering.

Yes! We offer catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact us at least one week in advance for catering inquiries. We can provide everything from casual buffets to formal sit-down dinners.

Yes, our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible. We have ramp access at the entrance, wide aisles, and accessible restrooms. Please let us know if you have any specific accessibility needs when making a reservation.

We kindly ask for at least 2 hours notice for reservation cancellations. For groups of 6 or more, we require 24 hours notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee.
